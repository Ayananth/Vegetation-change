USING NDVI

The main objective of this study is to detect the land cover changes using remote sensing data over a long period. The satellite images for the years 2008 and 2020 were downloaded and used for studying the changes in vegetation. The NDVI values are calculated from the data after extracting the study area to identify vegetated areas. Then the vegetated regions from the two data sets obtained after NDVI calculation are compared. We can see a great change in the area of vegetation from the two data sets. This study thus shows the destruction of vegetation through a time series analysis. This can be used for the analysis of the ongoing hazardous developments and can lead to proper planning of further developments in the area and to check other detrimental development plans.
